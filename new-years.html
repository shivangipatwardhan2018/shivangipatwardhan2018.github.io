Introduction

This year I am graduating university. Give this significant benchmark seemed like the perfect opportunity to set some goals and see them through.

Here are some goals I want to accomplish in 2018; these may change and I will add modifications as I go along. The goals are in terms of importance (in my opinion).

Goal 1: Continue to Learn.

One of the largest motivations behind this blog. The aim is to have this blog will be a quantitative summary of my day to day learning, analysis of the technical (or other) challenges I encounter and other inquisitiveness.

Goal 2: Break out of my confort zone.

Moving to Toronto in September and living in the big city, alone as an adult for the first time is going to be extreamly nerve wreaking. I hope to try something new, make new friends (the struggles are real) and enjoy my new home as the new girl in the city. Also see next goal.

Goal 3: Travel.

I am planning to travel alot this year, which has been on my to-do list for about 5 years now. After soo many years of dreaming, planning and saving up finally all seems to be in place to execute this plan of action. More on this later.

Goal 4: Read a book a week.

Reading as a huge passion of mine though high school, lost in the large stacks of assignments and exams. This year I hope to read a book a week; this can be a mix of technical books or story books. The first book so the year will be Sense and Sensibility by Jane Austen. 52 weeks, 52 books.
